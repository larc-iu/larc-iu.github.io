We are the the **L**inguistic **A**lgorithms, **R**esources, and **C**onservation group at Indiana University Bloomington's Department of Linguistics, led by https://lgessler.com[Prof. Luke Gessler].

We are united by our interest in algorithms and resources for computationally processing what are often called _low-resource_ or _endangered_ languages.
